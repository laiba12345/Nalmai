# AhaLoop name screen

Selected product name: **AhaLoop**

Positioning: AhaLoop detects confusion, recommends an evidence-informed teaching move, observes the next check, and improves strategy selection within the live lesson.

A web search performed on 19 July 2026 found no obvious exact-name education or software product. The words “Aha” and “loop” are used separately by established products and learning companies, so this is only a practical collision screen—not a trademark opinion or legal clearance. A formal trademark and domain review is required before commercial launch.

The former-name migration note is retained in the main README. Lowercase internal database, logger, and environment identifiers remain stable for backward compatibility and are not user-facing branding.
